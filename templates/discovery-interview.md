## 1. Organization Overview

### Company Context
- Tell me about [Company Name]. What does the company do?
- How many employees does the organization have?
- What's the organizational structure? (Divisions, departments, teams)
- What's your role and how long have you been here?

### Strategic Context
- What are the top 3 business priorities for the next 12 months?
- What's driving interest in AI/automation at this time?
- Have there been previous AI or automation initiatives? What happened?

---

## 2. Current Operations

### Process Landscape
- Walk me through a typical day/week in your division.
- What are the highest-volume activities? (Things done many times per day/week)
- What are the most time-consuming tasks?
- What tasks require the most specialized knowledge?

### Pain Points
- Where do things break down most often?
- What keeps you up at night operationally?
- If you could eliminate one task from your team's plate, what would it be?
- What do new hires struggle with most?

### Documentation
- How well-documented are your processes today? (Scale of 1-10)
- Where does documentation live? (Wiki, Google Docs, tribal knowledge)
- Who maintains documentation? How often is it updated?

---

## 3. Technology Landscape

### Current Systems
- What are the core systems your team uses daily?
- How do these systems connect to each other? (Manual, automated, not at all)
- What's your biggest technology frustration?

### Data & Access
- Where does your team's data live?
- Who has access to what systems?
- Are there data silos that cause problems?

### Technical Readiness
- Do your systems have APIs available?
- Do you have internal technical resources? (IT, engineering)
- What's the process for implementing new tools?

---

## 4. AI Readiness Assessment

### Current AI Usage
- Is anyone using AI tools today? (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.)
- What's working well? What's not?
- Are there policies around AI usage?

### Knowledge State
- How much institutional knowledge exists only in people's heads?
- What would happen if your top performer left tomorrow?
- Are there "experts" that everyone depends on?

### Change Appetite
- How does your organization typically handle change?
- What's the biggest obstacle to adopting new ways of working?
- Who would need to approve an AI initiative?

---

## 5. Success Criteria

### Desired Outcomes
- If we're successful, what does that look like in 6 months?
- What metrics would prove this was worthwhile?
- What would make this a failure?

### Constraints
- What's the budget range for this initiative?
- What's the timeline expectation?
- Are there any non-negotiables or red lines?

### Stakeholders
- Who else should I talk to as part of discovery?
- Who will be the executive sponsor?
- Who might resist this initiative? Why?

---

## 6. Quick Wins Identification

### Immediate Opportunities
- What's one thing that, if automated tomorrow, would make your life easier?
- Are there any repetitive tasks that don't require much judgment?
- What manual data entry exists that frustrates the team?

### Low-Hanging Fruit Criteria
- High volume (done many times)
- Low complexity (straightforward rules)
- Low risk (mistakes are easily corrected)
- High visibility (people would notice improvement)

---

## 7. Wrap-Up

### Next Steps
- What questions do you have for me?
- Who else should I speak with?
- What's the best way to schedule follow-ups?

### Materials Request
- Can you share any existing process documentation?
- Are there org charts or team structures available?
- Any previous assessments or audits I should review?
