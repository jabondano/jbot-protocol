## Section 5: Common Pitfalls (1-2 pages)

### Pitfall 1: Starting with Technology
*What to do instead:* Start with processes and knowledge

### Pitfall 2: Skipping Documentation
*What to do instead:* Invest in knowledge capture first

### Pitfall 3: Underinvesting in Change Management
*What to do instead:* Budget 3:1 for adoption support

### Pitfall 4: No Governance Framework
*What to do instead:* Define guardrails before deployment

### Pitfall 5: Trying to Boil the Ocean
*What to do instead:* Start with one division, one use case
